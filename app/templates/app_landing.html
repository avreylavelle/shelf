How Shelf Works

Search: Find titles and add them to Ratings, Reading List, or DNR.
Recommendations: Get tailored suggestions and tweak filters.
Ratings: Track what you’ve scored and update details.
Reading List: Manage planned and in‑progress reads.
DNR: Exclude titles you never want to see.
Navigation: Use the Dashboard tiles to move around the site.
