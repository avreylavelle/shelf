How recommendations work

Quick guide to the controls on this page:

Genres & Themes: Pick genres and themes to steer results.
Content types: Choose which formats to include (Manga, Manhwa, etc.).
Diversify: Spreads results across genres/themes and avoids near‑duplicate series.
Include less popular: Adds a small boost for lower‑popularity titles.
Personalized: Uses your ratings, clicks, and lists to bias results (requires enough history).
Earliest year: Bias toward newer titles (older titles get a small penalty).
